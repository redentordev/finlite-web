Our Purpose

Addressing financial literacy gap among Filipino youth and early earners

Through research surveys and stakeholder interviews, we identified that young Filipinos often struggle to manage personal finances due to complex traditional budgeting tools that overwhelm beginners.

The Solution:

FinLite combines AI receipt scanning, rule-based nudges, and beginner-friendly design to make money tracking simple and consistent.
