### 7.6 Support & Troubleshooting

**Q: The app won't open. What do I do?**
```
A: Try these steps:
1. Force close the app and reopen it
2. Restart your phone
3. Check that you have the latest version from the App Store/Google Play
4. Clear the app cache (Settings ‚Üí Apps ‚Üí FinLite ‚Üí Clear Cache)
5. If still not working, contact support@finlite.app
```

**Q: Receipt scanning isn't working. What's wrong?**
```
A: Make sure:
- You have internet connection
- Camera permissions are enabled for FinLite
- Receipt is well-lit and clearly visible
- The receipt isn't crinkled or faded
- Your app is up to date

Try taking the photo in different lighting or position. If the issue persists, 
email support@finlite.app with a screenshot of the receipt.
```

**Q: I'm not getting notifications. How do I fix this?**
```
A: Check:
1. Go to Settings ‚Üí Notifications
2. Verify notifications are enabled for FinLite
3. Check your phone's system notification settings
4. On iOS: Settings ‚Üí Notifications ‚Üí FinLite ‚Üí Allow Notifications
5. On Android: Settings ‚Üí Apps ‚Üí FinLite ‚Üí Permissions ‚Üí Notifications

If still not working, try uninstalling and reinstalling the app.
```
